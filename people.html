Meet Our Team

David Beswick BSc MRICS

Director

Stephen Magee BSc MRICS

Director

As owners of the Practice, David and Stephen are responsible for generating business, stimulating growth, retaining Clients and overseeing successful delivery.

Stephen has been with the Practice since 1993 and gained valuable experience across all sectors under the tutelage of Roy Naylor and Martin Devlin. Stephen became a Director in 2004.

David joined the Practice in 2004 and was quickly identified as a key driver of the Practice’s expansion. He became co-owner in 2017.

In their continued time together, they have successfully complemented each other’s strengths and weaknesses which has resulted in significant growth and expansion. They now manage several of Northern Ireland’s leading Property Portfolios and work extensively across the Public Sector. Both offer Project Management services to select Clients and have built longstanding relationships with several of NI’s leading Property Developers.
